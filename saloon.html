Share Leasing

Rent out your tokens for a period to another wallet, at an agreed fee, they will collect all rewards during that period. Get future rewards today.

Whats share leasing? and why?


We have a 90% Sell Tax to discourage liquidating into the LP, but sometimes holders want to just get a small amount of liquidity from their tokens, and aggregated token rewards could be enough to cover it.

Thats where Share Leasing comes in, you can ask for future ETH rewards today, at a discount ofcourse since the other party needs a benefit. This means your estimates of what your rewards will be after a certain period, and the discount you are offering today leaves the taker in decent profit, how you calculate it is up to you.

This is a willing renter willing taker scenario, at an agreed price for a duration of time.


How to create a share lease:

  • You first have to delegate to the smart contract, the amount of tokens you want to rent out.
  • Next you create the share lease: the amount of tokens you want to use, the duration you want to rent out for, and the once of ETH payment you are asking from any takers.
  • Your share lease immidietly appears below with other listed vacant leases. You can cancel it any time before its taken.

    • Taker instructions:

  • You can scroll through the list of listed share leases below, before they are taken they have the "take lease" button.
  • You then sign the transaction, make sure your wallet has the minimum ETH being asked for the lease.
  • On confirmation the lease tokens are yours, and you can immidietly start claiming daily rewards from it to recoup your costs, and eventually profit.
  • You can view your lease on the portfolio dashboard, and claim or conclude it from there.

    • Share Bidding ~ coming soon.

    > Share Bidding is designed to create arbitrage opportunities & allow investors to cashout without affecting the liquidity pool. This protects holders who don't want to sell yet, and those permanently invested.

    > By definition, its an alternative market place, for buying and selling tokens. With unclaimed or claimed rewards included in the price. Priced at a future value and date, but discounted for sell today.

    > Share bidding transactions are taxed.

    > Our 90% Sell tax on regular days acts as the doorman that checks your ticket/pass. Share leasing uses Discounted Cash Flows (DCF) to allow you to cash out total future value, today.

    > This involves coming up with a good estimate of future token value, in order to strike a good deal today.

    > Share bidding works best with wallets that have unclaimed rewards, otherwise the discount should reflect the "Ask Price" is for future value of tokens only. Rewards unclaimed will be reflected in the website lease lists soon.

    1. As seller, you transfer your holdings and its rewards to another party, at an agreed price between parties. Price is calculated at a discount, i.e. future value of tokens & expected rewards/proceeds at today's price. The taker is willing to take your holdings entity as is, and to continue running it to get full value. So, the bigger the discount a taker gets, the more the profit in the future.

    2. Seller is cashed out as soon as buyer transfers asked amount. The buyer now has to run the entity of holdings and claim all rewards and benefits until full value is reached.
        3. Example ~
        > Selling 5 million GUN tokens worth $10,000
        > Earning $200 weekly in rewards.
        > Listing value ~ $14,000.
        > Future Pricing Date ~ 7 months ahead.
        > Future Pricing Value ~ $15,600

      • Deposit tokens into Share Bidding smart contract. First thing is first you have to calculate a future value of your tokens using a reasonable rate.
      • As Seller, create a DCF contract with: Future Pricing Date, Future Value Estimate, Monthly Discount Rate , Fair Value Today based on discount rate
      • Buyer comes and also calculates the figures to check for fair value. If favourable, the taker takes up the offer by depositing $104,000 USDC to contract.
      • The USDC is straight away transferred to the DCF Seller, whilst our smart contract collects a fee. Rate is based on Fee Collector Rate in Main smart contract.
      • Tokens are also then transferred to the Buyer straight away. Buyer now has the duty to manage and claim all rewards due until the full value is recovered.
      • The difference with buying from Uniswap, as the buyer, is clearly the discounts. For the sellers, its the quick liquidation process, and at full value and tax free. Both clear advantages.
