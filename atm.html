Russian Roulette

Play Russian Roulette with the tax man. You have 1 in six chances he dies, pick a barrel for him and he pulls the trigger. If he dies, you can sell Tax Free on RR Day(each Saturday). Play once each month.

Russian Roulette is designed to limit the number of people who can sell tokens each month. Statistically each holder has a 1 in 6 chance each month. With the 90% sell tax in place, this creates a sell pressure bottle neck.

This helps us prepare for the buy backs in advance, since we know the number of sellers scheduled each month, and deploy excess funds accordingly.

The punishment is heavy for anyone who sells out of turn. Beat the odds in Russian Roulette, or stake for 6 months in order to get a 10% sell tax.

After each sell we want to restore the liquidity pool to the level it was before, so the next person benefits the same.


Rules:

  • You can only play once each month.
  • Wallet needs to hold at least 1000 Tokens to play.
  • You have a 1 in 6 chance of winning. Pick a Barrel and pull the Trigger.
  • Front-end submits odds 1 - 2 to the Smart Contract, Smart Contract has 1 - 3 odds to match your barrel choice.
  • If you kill the tax man, you win, you are in the tax free array. On R.R day (each Saturday) you can then sell tax with 10% tax only.
  • Your sell tax status is reflected on the Swaps Card Infor before any trade.
  • If you lose, you have to wait for a new month to begin in order to play again.
  • You win, you can hold your winning status for as long as you want and choose any day in the future to use it, your winning status will not be changed. The moment you decide to sell, it then resets.
  • Fee collectors cannot play Russian Roulette.
  • Wallet to wallet token transfer is restricted to almost zero throughput in order to prevent cheating, moving tokens to another wallet to play again.
