who are we and what we do:

Russian Roulette is a community hedge concept, a ponzi, that also thrives during the bull season. Here nothing is static ~ everything is responsive to market cycles and the subsequent buy or sell pressures. Its comprised of 3 contracts: Main, Rebalancing Wallet, and Treasury. The rebalancing wallet is a community controlled contract responsible to for responsively allocating fees where needed. To maximise on value containment, the primary goal of the RBW contract is to attempt to use buybacks to replace all eth lost from the liquidity pool. It automatically tracks the value of tokens scheduled for a sell and keeps a reserve for buybacks, sending only the excess to treasury. During the bear season our sell taxes are a radical 90% to discourage selling. Unless you schedule a sell then its 10% sell tax. But during the bull season we lower taxes based on fees collected to RBW. Taxes are not lowered blindly because the Rebalancing Bias will be blown out of range as holders take profit during the bull season, pushing the NVL higher. Bringing it down after so the RBW can function normally within ranges during the bear season would be hard since fees accured at this time will be insignificant compared to the unchecked NVL. So we remain responsive to the trading volume and lower to what we can cover, such that the rbb is always within a healthy and functional range. The scheduling of sells is left to chance through Russian Roulette. The value containment responsive nature of this project is what makes it a hedge, and its designed to be undumpable, and present the real first Up Only scenario.
This is a ponzi. We exist to explore sustainability in the crypto space, specifically for no-use-case projects. With the hope to see similar mechanisms developed or adopted across the industry to create a safe sustainable enviroment for investors. In this experiment we want to explore the effects of extreme taxing and buybacks, coupled with limited batched sells, on economic sustainability. In taxed txs projects, the trading volume and inherently taxes collected are the only income stream, and its worth preserving that life line. No project can simultaneously withstand unlimited value drain via unchecked sells whilst guaranteeing profit for all investors regardless of entry price, except possibly during the bull season when theres little to no damage to the project. Rebasing tokens tried guaranteeing value with an inflationary approach and failed. Staking mechanisms which were once viewed as the holy grail of managing sell pressure came and failed to do so. Now we have taxes. The goal is to contain value within the project, long enough for the project to achieve its goals or become TBTF. The goal is not just passive income but to also guarantee token value retention for all holders.
All taxes are collected in tokens on the contract. They are not liquidated through the liquidity pool as this defeats the whole point, they are liquidated through proxy-buys using our contract. We offer a 5-10% discount on tokens bought through the proxy dex as you will be helping liquidate fee tokens. The buy tax is a constant 15% but can be lower if the fee liquidation discount is factored in, thats if there are fee tokens available to meet your buy request. Once converted to ETH, all the income is automatically send to the Rebalancing Wallet, which is a community controlled contract, resposible for making buybacks or treasury allocations(another wallet), responsively to the buy and sell pressures. There are no blind allocations, the health of the project is guaged on Net Value Lost from the Liquidity pool, compared to buybacks, to give a RBB (rebelancing bias). The rebalancing bias is used by the contract to automatically trigger a buyback if the RBB is out of the healthy range, below 1 or above 1.5. Any community member can trigger this, when in a healthy range the contract then switches to allocations based on poll outcome. Each poll is time limited, and minimum voter checked, and on conclusion automatically allocates funds to where the community voted for, an automatic buy back or sending to treasury contract. There is no owner intervention whatsover on the Rebalancing Wallet.
Though theres a 90% tax in place (variable remember) its not a honeypot, holders can actually sell whenever they want into our Uniswap Liquidity Pool, just through our contract as a proxy. Most people are used to losing 90% or more of their holdings anyway in shit projects, so if investors want a quick way out at the expense of long term holders, we expect them to take the 90% loss. Instead of selling out of schedule we encourage the use of Share Leasing where you can rent out your tokens to another party and get a net equivalent or estimate today of all rewards expected during the lease period. In addition, Share bidding will be introduced which facilitates transfer of ownership outside of the LP using a bid and offer approach in fixed lots. The proxy dex will compete by offering discounts on fee tokens to lure buyers back to the Uniswap liquidity pool. Normally when people sell frantically it harms the holders, here it harms only the sellers, and leave the holders in a better place. In summary, with these new tokenomics, we are aiming to: guarantee value where Rebasing mechanisms failed to, and effectively manage sell pressure where Staking mechanisms failed to.

What's a Rebalancing wallet?

Its a community controlled wallet (smart contract), that acts as the intermediary to receive eth obtained from fees, then determine the beneficial way to allocate them based on sell and buy pressures. Other projects normally have a fixed amount that just goes to the treasury wallet, and to buy backs, with no actual justification for allocating the amounts. Based on trading volume it may be too little, it may be too much deployed to the treasury/buy backs.

How does a Rebalancing wallet work?

Since there is a trade off between keeping a healthy token price and growing the treasury wallet, we measure buy pressure and sell pressure to determine what to prioritize between buybacks and growing our treasury.
If the buy pressure is high all excess after "sufficient buybacks" is send to the treasury wallet (contract). If the sell pressure is high, net eth lost is high and we step up allocations towards buybacks.
How is this done?
The Rebalancing wallet uses net eth outflows from the LP, versus the net buybacks in eth ever. NVL/buybacks = Rebalancing bias (RBB level). Ideally an RBB of 1 is perfect, but not realistic all the time. So the wallet is programmed to keep an eye on the minimum RBB 1, Median RBB 1.25, and max RBB 1.5 to determine how much to allocate towards buybacks and afterwards, how much to send to the treasury wallet. This is done automatically. This is a true self governance project, the community controls the RB wallet, the owner has no control over theses funds whatsoever, the automated checks in place are enough. So there is a polling range (between 1 & 1.5) and an auto-rebalancing range (below 1 or above 1.5). Whilst in the former, any community member can automatically trigger a rebalance. When in the latter range, any community member can trigger a poll for the community to vote on where to send funds. On poll conclusion it allocates funds automatically based on the poll results.
